Kalitungan Falls are located at Brgy. San Juan Pasuquin, Ilocos Norte. A beautiful discovered where it will give you more than enough amazing things to see like gigantic moss-covered trees, wild orchids, colorful mushrooms and clear water. The cold water coming from the mountainside brings your body from freezing. It is one of the enjoyable destinations in Pasuquin because of its cascading falling water.


Sexy Beach, Pasuquin, Philippines This is one of the most famous beach in Pasuquin, Ilocos Norte, Philippines.
The Name Sexy Beach is derived from the shape of its shore forming like the "S" shape of a woman.

Along the highway a few kilometers north of the town of Pasuquin, there are roadside stalls selling bags of what is arguably the finest salt in the Philippines. It is a common sight to see people riding in provincial buses that often stop at these stalls to purchase salt. Sold by the kilo and contained in plastic bags, the salt is cooked in large vats in huts which are usually found behind the stalls.
